Welcome to history

"Human history in essence is the history of ideas.">-H.G. Wells

Hello, and welcome my name is Makayla Cambiano, and I am a graduate student at the University of Missouri, studying library science. I have a B.A.in World History, where I specialized in classics and antiquity. I graduated with honors in the Spring of 2023 from the University of Missouri-Kansas City.

I love to research and consider myself a lifelong learner with a passion for the pursuit of knowledge. I have worked in Museums and libraries, and now I continue my education as I travel the world trying to quench my thirst for knowledge. In high school, I had a deep passion for studio art and art history. I have had my work displayed in Jefferson City, Missouri in the State Capital building and I continue to make art commissions often. History is a huge part of my life and I hope to share some knowledge with this page. Thank you!
